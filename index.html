The main goal of the game is to shoot as many ducks as possible, while spending as few rounds of ammunition.To shoot you need to click the mouse on the screen, trying to hit the duck. One game consists of 5 waves. On each subsequent wave, the number of ducks and ammunition increases. The speed of the duck also increases. At the end of the game, the final statistics with the percentage of accuracy appear.The best record will be displayed in the menu depending on the level of difficulty.The number of ducks is equal to the number of rounds.The wave ends when the bullets or ducks run out.
